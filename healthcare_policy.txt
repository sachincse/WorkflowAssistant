CONFIDENTIAL: Healthcare Benefits Policy - Effective 2026

---
**COMPREHENSIVE HEALTH INSURANCE COVERAGE**

**Eligibility**
- All full-time employees are eligible from Day 1
- Coverage extends to spouse and up to 2 dependent children
- Pre-existing conditions covered after 6 months

**Coverage Details**
- **Hospitalization**: Up to $100,000 per year
- **Out-Patient**: Up to $5,000 per year
- **Dental**: Up to $2,000 per year
- **Vision**: Up to $500 per year
- **Mental Health**: Up to $3,000 per year (10 sessions)
- **Maternity**: Full coverage up to $15,000

**Network Providers**
- Primary: HealthCare Plus Network (500+ hospitals)
- Secondary: Global Medical Alliance (international coverage)
- Emergency: All hospitals accepted (reimbursement within 30 days)

**Enrollment Process**
1. Submit health insurance enrollment form within 7 days
2. Provide blood group and emergency contact information
3. Nominate beneficiaries
4. Upload dependent documents (if applicable)
5. Receive insurance card within 14 days

**Required Documents for Enrollment**
- Employee ID proof
- Blood group certificate
- Emergency contact details
- Dependent birth certificates (if applicable)
- Marriage certificate (for spouse coverage)

**Claims Process**
- Cashless: Use insurance card at network hospitals
- Reimbursement: Submit claims within 30 days with original bills
- Processing time: 7-14 business days

**Wellness Programs**
- Annual health checkup (free)
- Gym membership subsidy: $50/month
- Mental health counseling: Free
- Vaccination programs: Free (flu, COVID, etc.)

**MANDATORY ACTIONS**
- Insurance enrollment must be triggered automatically during onboarding
- Blood group and emergency contact must be collected
- Healthcare provider selection must be completed within 7 days
