**HR Relocation Policy**
- **Senior Engineers**: Eligible for **Tier-2 Relocation Package** (Includes Flight + 14 days Hotel stay).
- **Managers**: Eligible for **Tier-1 Relocation Package** (Business Class Flight + 30 days Serviced Apartment).
- **Junior Engineers**: Eligible for **Tier-3 Relocation Package** (Train/Bus + 7 days Hostel).

**Training Requirements**
- **All Employees**: Mandatory completion of "POSH" (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) training within 30 days of joining.
- **Male Employees**: Mandatory "Gender Sensitization" workshop.
- **Female Employees**: Mandatory "Safety Labs" workshop.

**Remote Work Eligibility**
- Remote work is permitted only if the `Remote_Allowed` flag is returned as `True` from the HR API.
- Default policy is Hybrid (3 days in office).
